About Us

The Zeta Upsilon chapter of Alpha Chi Omega was founded at Case Western Reserve University on September 24, 1983. Over the past thirty years, our chapter has grown to include over 90 active sisters with alumni throughout Cleveland and across the country. Within our diverse sisterhood, we support each other academically and socially, as well as through leadership and community involvement.

Philanthropy

We're very proud of our philanthropy, domestic violence awareness, and it's perhaps what we're best known for on campus. We directly benefit the Domestic Violence & Child Advocacy Center here locally in Cleveland. Each semester we hold events to raise awareness and funds, including DVA Week every Fall and Walk A Mile In Her Shoes every Spring. Check out our philanthropy page to learn more!

Recruitment

Case Western holds formal recruitment each Spring for all girls looking to go Greek. Visit our recruitment page and be sure to sign up for formal recruitment if you're interested!
