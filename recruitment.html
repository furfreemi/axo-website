Recruitment

Spring Formal Recruitment

Case sororities go through a formal recruitment process, taking place in Thwing, Tink, and the surrounding buildings over the first two weekends of the spring semester. For all the technical rules and details regarding recruitment, check out the Panhellenic Recruitment Rules.

Registration is required for recruitment, so make sure you sign up!


A Message from our VP of Recruitment


Dear Potential New Member,

Our chapter provides its members with friendship, new experiences, academic support, philanthropic opportunities, and much more. Joining Greek life here at Case Western is a fantastic way to meet new women who are involved in a diverse range of on-campus and off-campus activities.

I hope that you will consider participating in the unique and unforgettable experience that is Formal Recruitment in January.

The sisters of Alpha Chi Omega look forward to meeting you and welcoming you to the Greek community at CWRU.

Sincerely,
Laura Huber, former Vice President of Recruitment

For more information and to register for recruitment, please visit the Sorority Recruitment Information page.

Questions?

Recruitment can be a confusing process. Feel free to contact Shenali Warnakulasuriya, our VP of Recruitment, with any questions!
